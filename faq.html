Check WhatsApp Status Effortlessly

Stay connected with real-time status updates!

Our website is designed to help you boost the views on your WhatsApp status. Here's how it works:
  • First, you upload your contact number to our system.
  • We check if the uploaded number is registered on WhatsApp.
  • Once verified, your contact will be stored and linked with other users who have also uploaded their numbers.
  • After that, you can download a VCF (vCard) file, which contains the contact information of all mutual users.
  • You can then import this VCF file to your phone, enabling you to add these contacts and view their WhatsApp statuses.
This creates a mutual relationship where you can view each other's statuses on WhatsApp.

Uploading your contact is simple. Follow these steps:
  1. Navigate to the Upload Contact page from the navigation bar.
  2. Enter your phone number in the provided input field.
  3. Click the Submit button.
  4. Our system will verify if your contact is registered on WhatsApp.
  5. If verification is successful, your contact will be added to the community list, and you can proceed to the next step.
Remember, each user is only allowed to upload one contact. Make sure to provide the correct phone number.

After you successfully upload your contact, here’s what happens next:
  1. Our system checks if the number you submitted is registered with WhatsApp.
  2. Once verified, your contact is saved in the system, and you'll be added to the list of mutual contacts.
  3. You will receive a notification or prompt that the process was successful.
  4. You can now go to the Download VCF page to get the list of contacts shared by other users.
Once these steps are completed, you can move on to downloading the VCF file and adding the contacts to your phone.

Downloading the VCF (vCard) file is straightforward:
  1. Head to the Download VCF page once your contact has been uploaded and verified.
  2. Click on the Download button, which will generate a VCF file.
  3. This file contains the contact details of all users who have uploaded their numbers to the system.
  4. Save the VCF file to your device.
After downloading, you’ll need to import this VCF file to your phone to add the new contacts.

Here’s how to import the VCF (vCard) file to your phone:
  1. Once the VCF file is downloaded, locate it in your phone’s file manager.
  2. Tap on the file, and your phone will prompt you to Import Contacts.
  3. Confirm the import process. The new contacts will now be added to your phone’s contact list.
  4. After importing, open WhatsApp, and you’ll be able to view the statuses of those new contacts.
The import process may vary slightly depending on your phone’s operating system, but most smartphones support VCF imports natively.

To manage your contacts on the site, follow these steps:
  1. Go to your Profile page, where you’ll see the contact you’ve uploaded.
  2. You can choose to edit the contact’s name, if necessary, by clicking the Edit button.
  3. If you no longer wish to be part of the community, you can delete the contact by clicking the Delete button.
  4. Once deleted, you’ll no longer be able to view other users’ statuses, and they won’t be able to view yours.

Yes, you can leave the community at any time:
  1. Go to your Profile page and select the Delete Contact option.
  2. This will remove your contact from the system, and you will no longer be able to view or be viewed by other users on WhatsApp.
Once your contact is removed, you’ll need to re-upload it if you wish to rejoin in the future.
